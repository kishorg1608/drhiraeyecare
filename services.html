Eye Surgery

Cataract Surgery

Cataract is the leading cause of preventable blindness. Our surgeons use the latest phacoemulsification technique with premium Intraocular Lens (IOL) implantation to restore clear, natural vision. Performed in our modern, fully equipped operation theatre.

Eye Specialist

Glaucoma Detection & Management

Glaucoma is the "silent thief of sight" — causing irreversible damage before symptoms appear. We offer comprehensive screening using tonometry, visual field analysis, and advanced imaging. Early detection prevents permanent vision loss.

IOP MeasurementVisual FieldOCT ImagingLaser Treatment

Retina Care

Diabetic Eye Screening & Retina Care

Diabetes can silently damage retinal blood vessels. We conduct fundus photography and fluorescein angiography for early detection of diabetic retinopathy, with laser or injection-based treatment to protect your vision.

Pediatric Eye Care

Children's eyes are still developing and need specialist attention. We treat refractive errors, amblyopia (lazy eye), squint (strabismus), and congenital conditions in a calm, child-friendly environment. Early treatment makes all the difference.
