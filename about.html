Our Founder

Dr. Chayan Hira

Founder, Chairman, Ophthalmologist & Managing Director

Nestled within the heart of Chakdah, Dr. Hira Eyecare & Diagnostic Centre was established in 2025 by Dr. Chayan Hira alongside Co-founder Dr. Piyasa Bhattacharya. The centre was built on a simple yet powerful philosophy — "Better Vision for a Better Life."

As an ophthalmologist, Dr. Chayan Hira completed approximately 2,500 microsurgeries (SICS + PHACO) while pursuing his postgraduate degree, including single-handedly performing 22 SICS surgeries. This depth of surgical experience forms the foundation of the clinical excellence at Dr. Hira Eyecare.

Our philosophy is to eliminate blindness by providing compassionate and high-quality eye care to all — regardless of social or economic background.

Founder's Message

A Message from Our Founder

Dr. Hira Eyecare (Eye Hospital) was founded with a simple yet powerful vision inspired by Dr. Chandrashekhar Hira — to provide "Clear Vision, Beautiful Life" to every member of society.

This institution was born out of a deep sense of responsibility and compassion toward the community. The idea behind establishing Dr. Hira Eyecare was not just to create a medical facility, but to build a centre where people can receive reliable, ethical, and advanced eye care services.

We strongly believe that "Healthy Eyes Lead to a Brighter Future." Guided by this philosophy, our centre strives to provide quality eye care for everyone, regardless of social or economic background. Every patient who walks through our doors deserves the best possible treatment, attention, and care.

Today, Dr. Hira Eyecare is more than just a hospital — it represents a commitment to restoring vision, improving eye health, and bringing light back into people's lives. What started as a dream has now become a meaningful journey dedicated to helping individuals see the world more clearly.

Our team of experienced ophthalmologists, optometrists, and vision care professionals work together with modern diagnostic technology to ensure accurate diagnosis and effective treatment for a wide range of eye conditions.

We remain committed to expanding our services, embracing modern medical advancements, and continuing our mission of delivering trusted and compassionate eye care to the community.
